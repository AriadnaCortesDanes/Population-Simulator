Population simulator

How to use?

This simulator has 3 modes.

By clicking the "Infinite food" button, a simulation will start where bunnies have access to infinite food.

By clicking "Limited food", a simulation will start where the bunnies have limited food and therefore die or reproduce depending on how much food they get.

By clicking "Predator", a simulation will start where not only bunnies have limited food but also there are wolfs which try to eat bunnies.
